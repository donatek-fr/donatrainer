Command & Control Center

  • AN - **Availability Neutral:** Searches for flight availability. (AN15DECFRAJFK)
  • SS - **Sell Segment:** Books a seat from the availability display. (SS1Y1)
  • NM - **Name:** Adds passenger names to the booking. (NM1SMITH/JOHN MR(CHD))
  • AP/APE - **Address/Phone/Email:** Adds contact information. (APE J.SMITH@MAIL.COM)
  • TKOK - **Ticket OK:** Confirms that the PNR will be ticketed.
  • RF - **Received From:** Records who made the booking. (RF J SMITH)
  • ER - **End & Retrieve:** Saves the PNR and assigns a Record Locator.
  • RT - **Retrieve:** Displays a PNR by its locator or redisplays the current one. (RT JX7K9P)
  • IR / IG - **Ignore & Retrieve:** Discards any unsaved changes and redisplays the PNR.
  • RH - **Retrieve History:** Displays the change log for the current PNR.
  • NU - **Name Update:** Corrects or changes a passenger name. (NU1/1SMITH/JONATHAN MR)
  • XE - **Cancel Element:** Removes a specific element. (XE2)
  • SP - **Split PNR:** Separates specific passengers into a new booking. (SP 2,3)
  • RM - **Remark:** Adds a general, non-automated note to the PNR. (RM CLIENT IS A VIP)
  • SSR - **Special Service Request:** Adds standardized requests. (SSR DOCS ...)
  • FXP - **Fare Quote Price:** Prices the itinerary and creates a stored fare (TST).
  • FQN - **Fare Quote Note:** Displays the rules for the stored fare.
  • TTP - **Ticket PNR:** Issues the electronic ticket for the stored fare.
  • TWX - **Ticket Work Void:** Voids a ticket on the same day it was issued.
  • TRF - **Ticket Refund:** Processes a refund for a ticket.
  • ITR/P - **Itinerary Print:** Displays and prints the full itinerary/e-ticket receipt.
  • PROFILE - **Client Profile:** Creates or loads a client's saved data into a PNR. (PROFILE LOAD JANE DOE)
  • FXA / FXK - **Ancillary Services:** Displays and prices available extras like baggage.
  • TTP/EMD - **Issue EMD:** Issues a separate ticket for a paid ancillary service.
  • FCM - **Fare Commission/Markup:** Adds an agency service fee. (FCM-A20)
  • QP / QT / QS - **Queue Management:** Places, views, and works on PNRs in agency queues. (QP/1/1)
  • HE - **Help:** Displays a list of primary commands.
  • CS - **Clear Screen:** Clears the terminal display.
  • DNA - **Decode Airline:** Looks up an airline by its two-letter code. (DNA BA)
  • DACH - **Decode Airport:** Looks up an airport by its three-letter code. (DACH LHR)
